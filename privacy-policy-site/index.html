Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

At KJR Labs, we believe that your data belongs to you. The Quick Symbols Chrome extension was built with a "Privacy by Design" approach. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

2. No Data Collection

Quick Symbols does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • We do not track your browsing history.
  • We do not read the content of the websites you visit.
  • We do not collect your search queries or keyboard inputs.
  • We do not use any analytics or tracking scripts.

3. Local Storage Only

The extension utilizes local browser storage (chrome.storage.local) to save your "Favorite" and "Recently Used" symbols. This data remains strictly on your local machine and is never synced to the cloud or shared with KJR Labs.

4. Permissions Used

Quick Symbols requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • clipboardWrite: Used exclusively to copy the selected symbol to your clipboard so you can paste it into your documents.
  • storage: Used to persist your favorites and history locally for a better user experience.

5. Third-Party Services

Quick Symbols does not communicate with any external servers. There are no third-party APIs, advertisements, or tracking pixels integrated into the extension.
